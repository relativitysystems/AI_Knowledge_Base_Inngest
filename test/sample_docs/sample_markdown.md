# Relativity Systems — Service Pricing Guide

Last updated: 2026-06-01

## Overview

Relativity Systems offers AI automation and integration services for small to medium businesses. All pricing below is in USD and reflects monthly retainer rates unless otherwise noted.

---

## Service Packages

### Starter — $1,500 / month

Includes:
- Up to 3 automated workflows (n8n or Inngest)
- 1 AI integration (OpenAI, Claude, or Gemini)
- Monthly check-in call (30 min)
- Email support (48-hour response SLA)

Best for: businesses new to AI automation that want a low-risk starting point.

### Growth — $3,500 / month

Includes:
- Up to 10 automated workflows
- Up to 3 AI integrations
- RAG knowledge base setup (1 client, 1 document source)
- Bi-weekly check-in calls (45 min)
- Slack support channel (24-hour response SLA)
- Monthly performance report

Best for: growing businesses that need multiple automations and are ready for AI-powered Q&A.

### Enterprise — Custom pricing

Includes:
- Unlimited workflows
- Multi-source AI knowledge base (Google Drive, Dropbox, Notion, web crawl)
- Custom AI agent development
- Weekly strategy calls
- Dedicated Slack support (4-hour response SLA)
- Priority access to new Relativity features

Contact Relativity Systems for a custom quote.
